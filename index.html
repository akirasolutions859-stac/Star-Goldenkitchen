Who We Are

Bringing Innovation, Purity, & Comfort To Your Doorstep

Based in Ankurhati, Howrah, Star Golden Kitchen & Home Appliances is your trusted local partner for home improvement and clean water.

We began with a clear mission: to provide families and businesses in West Bengal with crystal-clear, healthy water and beautifully optimized spaces. Over the years, we have integrated deep technical expertise in domestic & industrial water purification with high-end modular interior designs.

Whether you need a simple filter service for your home Aquaguard, a heavy-duty 1000 Liters/Hour commercial RO plant for your factory, or a stunning false ceiling and modular kitchen layout, we deliver precision, durability, and outstanding value.
